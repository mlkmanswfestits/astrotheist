Cristiano Ronaldo

Born February 5, 1985 • Funchal, Portugal

The Chart Ruler

With a Capricorn Ascendant, Ronaldo is ruled by Saturn. This is the planet of discipline, hard work, and ambition.

It is no surprise that he is known for his insane work ethic. Capricorn Risings age in reverse; they get better with time. Saturn placement (often in Scorpio for his generation) adds intensity and focus. He treats his body like a machine (Capricorn/Saturn) to achieve dominance.

Key Placements

  • Sun in Aquarius: The team player who stands out.
  • Moon in Leo: The need for applause, glory, and being "The Best."
  • Ascendant in Capricorn: The disciplined professional.
  • Mars in Aries: Pure, explosive physical power and competitiveness.

Detailed Analysis

♂

Mars in Aries

This is Mars in its dignity (home). It gives him raw speed, aggression, and a "me first" attitude on the pitch. He is a striker who instinctively knows where the goal is. It gives him unlimited energy reserves.

☽

Moon in Leo

He thrives on the "Siuuu!" chant. Leo Moons need to be adored. They are proud and have a regal air. They perform best when the spotlight is on them. It also makes him a generous father and leader.

☉

Sun in Aquarius

Despite his Leo ego, his core is Aquarius. He can be detached and cool under pressure. He understands the mechanics of the game intellectually. He is also unique—there is no one quite like him.
