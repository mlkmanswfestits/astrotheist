Bob Marley

Born February 6, 1945 • Nine Mile, Jamaica

The Chart Ruler

With a Sagittarius Ascendant, Bob was ruled by Jupiter. This is the planet of faith, music, philosophy, and global expansion.

A Sagittarius Rising explains his role as a spiritual prophet. He wasn't just a singer; he was a preacher of One Love. Jupiter expands whatever it touches, and for Bob, it expanded his message (and his family—Saturn in Cancer indicated many children) to the entire world.

Key Placements

  • Sun in Aquarius: The visionary, the brother of all mankind.
  • Moon in Scorpio: Deep, intense, transformative emotions. Private suffering.
  • Ascendant in Sagittarius: The global messenger, the Rastaman.
  • Uranus rising in Gemini: The revolutionary voice that sparked change.

Detailed Analysis

☉

Sun in Aquarius

Like many great agents of change, he was an Aquarius. He saw humanity as one tribe. He was detached from material wealth in many ways ("Money can't buy life"). He stood for freedom above all else.

☽

Moon in Scorpio

This adds a heavy, mystical layer to his personality. He felt things intensely. He dealt with betrayal (attempted assassination) and death (cancer) with a transformative, almost shamanic power. It gave his music its "soul" and haunting quality.

↑

Sagittarius Rising

The Walker. The Traveller. The Believer. This placement gave him his distinctive look (wild hair, casual style) and his mission to spread a philosophy. He was a natural teacher.
