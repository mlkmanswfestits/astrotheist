Astrology Resources

Curated books, websites, and tools for your astrological journey

üìö Essential Books

The Inner Sky

by Steven Forrest

The best introduction to psychological astrology. Forrest explains chart interpretation with clarity, depth, and soul. Essential for beginners and a worthy re-read for advanced students.

Beginner Psychological Modern

Cosmos and Psyche

by Richard Tarnas

A masterwork connecting planetary cycles to historical events and collective consciousness. Dense but brilliant. Shows astrology's validity through rigorous historical analysis.

Advanced Mundane Philosophy

The Astrology of Fate

by Liz Greene

Jungian astrology at its finest. Greene explores the psychological dimensions of the chart, particularly Saturn, Pluto, and the outer planets. Deep and transformative.

Intermediate Jungian Psychological

Chiron and the Healing Journey

by Melanie Reinhart

The definitive work on Chiron. Explores the wounded healer archetype with psychological depth and practical wisdom. Essential for understanding core wounds in the chart.

Intermediate Chiron Healing

Christian Astrology

by William Lilly

The classic traditional astrology text from 1647. Dense and challenging, but the foundation of horary and traditional techniques. For serious students only.

Advanced Traditional Horary
