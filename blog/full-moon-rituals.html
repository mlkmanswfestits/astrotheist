Full Moon Rituals for Manifestation

Releasing the Old to Make Space for the New

January 5, 2026 • 5 min read

The moon cycles affect the tides of the ocean, and since human bodies are up to 60% water, it's no surprise they affect us too. The Full Moon is the high tide of the lunar cycle—a peak of energy, emotions, and illumination.

While the New Moon is a time for setting intentions (planting seeds), the Full Moon is a time for harvesting and releasing. It illuminates what is working and what is holding us back. By performing a simple ritual, you align your personal energy with this cosmic rhythm.

A Simple 3-Step Ritual

1. Purify

Cleanse your space with sage, palo santo, or sound. Take a salt bath to cleanse your aura.

2. Release

Write down everything you want to let go of on a piece of paper. Burn it safely (fire transforms).

3. Charge

Place your crystals or moon water under the moonlight to recharge their energy.

Tailoring Rituals to the Sign

Every Full Moon takes on the flavor of the zodiac sign it falls in. Knowing this allows you to target your magic:

  • Full Moon in Aries: Release anger and impatience.
  • Full Moon in Taurus: Release financial scarcity mindsets.
  • Full Moon in Gemini: Release gossiping and scattered thinking.
  • Full Moon in Cancer: Release family trauma and emotional insecurity.
  • Full Moon in Leo: Release the need for external validation.
  • Full Moon in Scorpio: Release grudges and toxic attachments.

The Importance of "Moon Water"

One of the easiest tools is Moon Water. Simply fill a glass jar with filtered water and set it outside (or on a windowsill) under the Full Moon. The water absorbs the lunar energy. You can then drink it, water your plants with it, or add it to your bath to embody that energy throughout the month.
