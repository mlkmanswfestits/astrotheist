Midheaven & Career Astrology

What Are You Meant to Be When You Grow Up?

December 5, 2025 • 7 min read

One of the most common questions astrologers get is: "What kind of job should I do?" For this, we look straight up to the very top of the chart—the Midheaven, or Medium Coeli (MC).

The MC represents your public reputation, your legacy, and the role you play on the world stage. It's not just "what you do for money" (that's the 2nd and 6th Houses). It's what you want to be known for.

Midheaven Through the Elements

Fire MC (Aries, Leo, Sagittarius)

Needs to lead, inspire, and create. Cannot stand being micromanaged.

  • Entrepreneur
  • Performer
  • Motivational Speaker

Earth MC (Taurus, Virgo, Capricorn)

Needs structure, tangible results, and mastery. Building something that lasts.

  • CEO / Manager
  • Architect
  • Financial Planner

Air MC (Gemini, Libra, Aquarius)

Needs communication, social interaction, and ideas. Connecting people and concepts.

  • Writer / Journalist
  • Lawyer / Mediator
  • Tech Innovator

Water MC (Cancer, Scorpio, Pisces)

Needs emotional connection, healing, and intuition. Caring for the soul of the world.

  • Therapist / Healer
  • Artist / Musician
  • Social Worker

The 10th House Rulers

To get specific, you must look at any planets residing in the 10th House, as well as the planet that rules your MC sign. For example, if you have a Gemini Midheaven, your career path is ruled by Mercury. If your Mercury is in the 5th House of Creativity, you might be a creative writer. If it's in the 8th House of Mystery, you might be an investigative journalist.
