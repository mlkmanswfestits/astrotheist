Angles & Points

The Structural Pillars of Your Cosmic Identity

The angles and points are the most personal parts of your natal chart. Unlike the planets, which show shared archetypal energies, the angles (calculated based on your exact birth time and location) anchor these energies to your physical existence and social destiny. They are the 4 cardinal directions of your life.

The Four Cardinal Angles

↑

The Ascendant (AC)

The "Rising Sign." Your mask, your physical body, and how you initiate action. The filter through which you view the world.
⟁

The Midheaven (MC)

Your public persona, career, legacy, and reputation. The highest point in your chart and your visible destiny.
⚓

The Imum Coeli (IC)

The "Bottom of the Sky." Your roots, ancestry, home, and deepest private self. The psychological foundation.
⇄

The Descendant (DC)

The gateway to others. Partnership, legal agreements, and the qualities you project onto or seek in "The Other."

Calculated Cosmic Points

Beyond the physical angles, astrology utilizes mathematical points that represent karmic flow and energetic abundance:

☊

Lunar Nodes

The North and South Nodes represent your karmic baggage and your soul's intended growth direction.

⊕

Part of Fortune

An Arabic Part representing where the Sun, Moon, and Ascendant harmonize for success and joy.
★

Fixed Stars

Powerful stellar influences like Algol and Regulus that add mythic intensity to your planet's placements.
⚷

Chiron

The Wounded Healer. A bridge between personal and transpersonal planets representing core wounds.

Why They Matter

Two people born on the same day will have the same planetary placements, but their angles will be completely different. The angles determine the houses—the specific stages where your planetary "actors" perform their roles. Without correct angles, a chart is generalized; with them, it is a surgical blueprint of your unique existence.
