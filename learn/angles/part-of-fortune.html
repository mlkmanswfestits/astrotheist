The Part of Fortune

The Pot of Gold • Joy • Material & Spiritual Wealth

Of all the "Arabic Parts" in ancient astrology, the Part of Fortune (Pars Fortunae) is the most powerful. It is the sweet spot in your chart where the Sun, Moon, and Ascendant align in perfect harmony to produce joy, abundance, and luck.

The Part of Fortune is not a planet. It is a calculated point. It represents the intersection of your physical body (Ascendant), your soul/emotions (Moon), and your spirit/will (Sun). When these three are in balance, "Fortune" is the result. It shows where luck comes to you and how you can find true happiness.

The Hidden Formula

The formula differs for day and night births, emphasizing the importance of the sect (day/night) of your chart.

  • Day Chart (Sun above horizon): Ascendant + Moon - Sun
  • Night Chart (Sun below horizon): Ascendant + Sun - Moon

1. Joy vs. Money

While often associated with money, the Part of Fortune is primarily about Joy. In ancient philosophy, having "Fortune" meant having a good life—which included health, prosperity, and contentment.

If you follow the path of your Part of Fortune, money often follows, because you are operating in a zone of natural ease and flow. It is the place where you don't have to "push" the river.

3. Part of Fortune in the Houses

The House placement tells you the area of life where this joy manifests.

  • 1st House: Luck through your own identity and charisma.
  • 2nd House: Luck through money and assets.
  • 3rd House: Luck through communication and siblings.
  • 4th House: Luck through real estate and family.
  • 5th House: Luck through gambling, kids, and art.
  • 6th House: Luck through work and health practices.
  • 7th House: Luck through marriage and partners.
  • 8th House: Luck through inheritance and other people's money.
  • 9th House: Luck through travel and foreign lands.
  • 10th House: Luck through career and fame.
  • 11th House: Luck through friends and social circles.
  • 12th House: Luck through spirituality and isolation.
