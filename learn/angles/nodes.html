The Lunar Nodes

The Dragon's Head & Tail • Karma • Purpose

While the planets describe what energies you have (e.g., "I have a temper" from Mars), the Lunar Nodes describe where you are coming from and where you are going. They are the vector of your soul's evolution.

The Lunar Nodes are not planets. They are mathematical points where the Moon's orbit intersects the Ecliptic (the Sun's apparent path). They always exist in exact opposition to each other, forming a polarity that defines your life's karmic lesson.

The Dragon in the Sky

In Vedic and ancient astrology, the Nodes are known as the Dragon (Rahu and Ketu).

  • North Node (Rahu / Dragon's Head): Insatiable hunger. The uncharted territory. Where we must go to grow.
  • South Node (Ketu / Dragon's Tail): Release. The familiar past. Where we are gifted but stagnant.

1. The South Node: Your Comfort Zone

The South Node (☋) represents your past lives, your childhood inheritance, and your innate talents. It is what you are already good at—so good, in fact, that you might be stuck there.

It is the "line of least resistance." Operating from your South Node feels easy, safe, and familiar. However, staying there too long leads to stagnation and a sense of futility. It is the "drain" of the chart where energy is lost if we do not move forward.

2. The North Node: Your True North

The North Node (☊) represents your destiny in this lifetime. It is the direction your soul wants to go, but feels terrified to approach.

The North Node feels foreign, awkward, and risky. It is a muscle you have never used before. But every time you move toward your North Node, the universe rewards you with growth, energy, and synchronicity. It is the "intake" valve of the chart.

3. Balancing the Polarity

The goal is not to abandon the South Node entirely, but to use its gifts to fuel the North Node journey.

Example: South Node in Capricorn / North Node in Cancer.

  • Past (SN Capricorn): You have lifetimes of experience being a boss, a leader, or a loner. You are naturally competent and self-reliant.
  • Destiny (NN Cancer): Your soul wants to learn emotional vulnerability, nurturing, and family connection.
  • Balance: Use your Capricorn competence to build a safe structure (SN) that protects and nurtures your family (NN). Don't just work yourself to death (Stagnant SN).

4. The Tribal Interpretations (Signs)

Aries NN / Libra SN

The Journey to Self. You have spent lifetimes merging with others, compromising, and keeping the peace (Libra). Now, you are here to learn courage, independence, and how to put yourself first (Aries). Mantra: "It is safe to be me."

Taurus NN / Scorpio SN

The Journey to Peace. You are used to crisis, intensity, and emotional turmoil (Scorpio). You are here to learn stability, simplicity, and how to enjoy the physical pleasures of life without drama (Taurus). Mantra: "I build slowly."

Gemini NN / Sagittarius SN

The Journey to Connection. You are used to being the guru, the nomad, or the philosopher (Sagittarius). You are here to learn how to listen, connect with your local community, and see the facts right in front of you (Gemini). Mantra: "I listen to learn."

Cancer NN / Capricorn SN

The Journey to Feeling. You are used to being the boss, the stoic, and the responsible one (Capricorn). You are here to learn how to cry, how to nurture, and how to define success emotionally rather than materially (Cancer). Mantra: "I allow myself to feel."

Leo NN / Aquarius SN

The Journey to the Heart. You are used to being the observer, the scientist, or the face in the crowd (Aquarius). You are here to learn how to take center stage, create from the heart, and risk being seen as special (Leo). Mantra: "I shine my light."

Virgo NN / Pisces SN

The Journey to Order. You are used to chaos, spirituality, and dissolving boundaries (Pisces). You are here to learn discernment, routine, physical health, and the sacredness of daily practice (Virgo). Mantra: "I serve through detail."

Libra NN / Aries SN

The Journey to Partnership. You are used to being the warrior, the loner, and the competitor (Aries). You are here to learn cooperation, diplomacy, and how to see the world through another's eyes (Libra). Mantra: "We differ, yet we are one."

Scorpio NN / Taurus SN

The Journey to Depth. You are used to safety, accumulation, and surface stability (Taurus). You are here to learn how to let go, how to merge deeply, and how to embrace the messy transformation of the soul (Scorpio). Mantra: "I embrace the unknown."

Sagittarius NN / Gemini SN

The Journey to Truth. You are used to gathering facts, gossip, and data (Gemini). You are here to learn intuition, faith, and the search for a higher, unifying meaning or philosophy (Sagittarius). Mantra: "I believe."

Capricorn NN / Cancer SN

The Journey to Mastery. You are used to staying in the safety of the nest, dependent on others emotionally (Cancer). You are here to learn self-mastery, public achievement, and how to be your own authority (Capricorn). Mantra: "I am the architect."

Aquarius NN / Leo SN

The Journey to the Collective. You are used to being the king, the star, and the center of attention (Leo). You are here to learn equality, collaboration, and how to serve a vision larger than your own ego (Aquarius). Mantra: "We rise together."

Pisces NN / Virgo SN

The Journey to Spirit. You are used to control, criticism, and anxiety over details (Virgo). You are here to learn surrender, faith, forgiveness, and the flow of the universe (Pisces). Mantra: "I let go."

5. Nodal Return and Nodal Opposition

The Nodes take approximately 18.6 years to circle the zodiac.

  • Nodal Return (Age ~18, 37, 55): The North Node returns to its natal position. A time of destiny. You are pushed onto your correct path. Events feel "fated."
  • Nodal Opposition (Age ~9, 27, 46): The Transiting North Node hits your South Node. A time of releasing the past. You may be forced to let go of old habits or karma.
