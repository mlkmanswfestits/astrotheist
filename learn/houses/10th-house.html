10th House

House of Career • Angular • Capricorn Energy

Public Image • Legacy • Authority

The 10th House is the house of career, public image, and legacy. It begins at the Midheaven (MC) and represents your professional path, reputation, and contribution to society. This is the "I achieve" house—your calling, status, and how the world sees you at your peak.

Core Meanings

  • Career: Your professional path, vocation, and life work
  • Public Image: Your reputation, how society sees you
  • Legacy: What you leave behind, your mark on the world
  • Authority: Your relationship with power, bosses, and status
  • Achievement: Success, recognition, and accomplishment
  • Contribution: How you serve the collective
  • Parent: Often the father or dominant parent figure

House Qualities

  • Type: Angular (action, initiation, power)
  • Natural Ruler: Capricorn
  • Natural Planetary Ruler: Saturn
  • Element: Earth (matter, manifestation)
  • Cusp: The Midheaven (MC)

Planets in the 10th House

Planets in the 10th house are highly visible in your public life. They shape your career and reputation:

☉ Sun in 10th House

Career is central to identity. Natural leader, seeks recognition and authority. Public success is life purpose. Can be workaholic or status-obsessed.

☽ Moon in 10th House

Emotional connection to career. Public image fluctuates. Nurturing or caregiving profession. Mother may be prominent or career-focused.

☿ Mercury in 10th House

Communication-based career. Intellectual reputation. Teaching, writing, or speaking professionally. Known for ideas and mental agility.

♀ Venus in 10th House

Artistic or beauty-related career. Diplomatic public image. Success through charm and aesthetics. May work in relationships, art, or luxury.

♂ Mars in 10th House

Ambitious, competitive career drive. Athletic or military profession. Assertive public image. Can be aggressive in pursuit of success.

♃ Jupiter in 10th House

Expansive career, natural success. Teaching, travel, or philosophical profession. Lucky reputation. Can overextend or promise too much.

♄ Saturn in 10th House (Domicile)

Saturn at home—serious career focus. Slow but steady climb to authority. Respected reputation. Can be burdened by responsibility or fear failure.

♅ Uranus in 10th House

Unconventional career path. Sudden changes in profession. Innovative reputation. Technology, astrology, or humanitarian work. Can be erratic or rebellious.

♆ Neptune in 10th House

Artistic, spiritual, or healing career. Idealized public image. Film, music, or charity work. Can be confused about path or have scandals.

♇ Pluto in 10th House

Powerful, transformative career. Intense public image. Psychology, research, or crisis work. Career involves death/rebirth cycles. Can be obsessed with power.

Signs on the 10th House Cusp (Midheaven)

Your MC sign shows your ideal career path and public calling:

  • Aries MC: Leadership, entrepreneurship, pioneering careers
  • Taurus MC: Finance, real estate, luxury, arts
  • Gemini MC: Writing, teaching, media, communications
  • Cancer MC: Caregiving, hospitality, counseling, food
  • Leo MC: Entertainment, leadership, creative arts, politics
  • Virgo MC: Healthcare, analysis, service, craftsmanship
  • Libra MC: Law, diplomacy, design, partnerships
  • Scorpio MC: Psychology, research, transformation, occult
  • Sagittarius MC: Teaching, travel, publishing, philosophy
  • Capricorn MC: Business, management, government, mastery
  • Aquarius MC: Technology, humanitarian work, innovation
  • Pisces MC: Arts, healing, spirituality, film, charity

10th House vs 4th House (IC)

The 10th and 4th houses are opposite and must be balanced:

  • 10th House: Public life, career, outer achievement, father
  • 4th House: Private life, home, inner foundation, mother

Overemphasis on the 10th creates workaholism and emotional emptiness. Balancing career with home life is essential.

Jungian Interpretation

Individuation: The 10th house represents your contribution to the collective unconscious. It's how you serve society and leave your mark.

Integration: Your career should be rooted in your emotional foundation (4th house). Success without inner peace is hollow.

Shadow Work for 10th House

Questions to explore:

  • Am I sacrificing personal life for career success?
  • Do I seek status for its own sake or authentic contribution?
  • How does my relationship with authority reflect my father?
  • What legacy do I truly want to leave?
  • Can I balance achievement with emotional connection?
