Life Areas & Psychological Stages

Action, initiation, and major life themes

1st House - Self

Ascendant, identity, appearance, persona, how you meet the world
4

IC, family, roots, private self, foundation, emotional security
7

7th House - Partnerships

Descendant, marriage, contracts, open enemies, projection
10

10th House - Career

Midheaven, public image, legacy, authority, vocation

Succedent Houses (2, 5, 8, 11)

Resources, values, and consolidation

2nd House - Resources

Money, possessions, values, self-worth, talents, security

Romance, children, play, self-expression, joy, risk

Siblings, neighbors, learning, short trips, daily mind

Higher education, travel, beliefs, meaning, expansion

12th House - Spirituality

Unconscious, solitude, sacrifice, hidden enemies, transcendence

Understanding House Systems

Houses divide the sky into 12 sections based on Earth's rotation. The most common systems are Placidus (unequal houses, time-based) and Whole Sign (each sign = one house). Your Ascendant always marks the 1st house cusp. Planets in houses show WHERE life themes play out; signs show HOW.
