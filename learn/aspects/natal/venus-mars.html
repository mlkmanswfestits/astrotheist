Venus-Mars Aspects

The Lovers

This is the Cosmic Couple. Venus is what you attract; Mars is what you chase. Venus is your desire for union; Mars is your desire for conquest. Together, they form the primary sexual signature of the birth chart.

This pair governs not only sex and romance but also creativity and artistry. It is the blending of Yin (Venus) and Yang (Mars). When they are in aspect, the person is driven by passion. To live without passion for a Venus-Mars type is a form of death.

The Archetype of Passion

Whether the aspect is hard or soft, there is a "pulse" to these people. They are magnetic. They exude a mix of softness and strength that is highly attractive to others.

They are often creatively gifted because art requires both the appreciation of beauty (Venus) and the technical skill/drive to execute it (Mars).

The Harmonious Aspects: Trine & Sextile

The Smooth Operator

With the Trine or Sextile, you have a natural ease with the opposite sex (and your own sexuality). You don't have to "try" to be attractive; you just are.

Your timing in love is excellent. You know when to make a move and when to pull back. You are generally successful in romance because you are clear about what you want, and your desires are not in conflict with your methods. You are a lover who can also be a best friend.

The Shadow: Laziness

Because attraction comes easily to you, you might not work hard at relationships. You might rely on your charm to get by, rather than doing the deep emotional work.

The Dynamic Aspects: Square & Opposition

The Love-Hate Dynamic (Square)

The Venus Square Mars is the aspect of "fatal attraction." You are drawn to people who are bad for you, or you find yourself in relationships that are high-drama and high-conflict.

Your timing is often off. You chase people who are unavailable, and when they finally want you, you lose interest. There is a deep dissatisfaction here—what you want (Venus) and what you do to get it (Mars) are at cross-purposes. However, this creates intense sexual chemistry. You are never boring.

The War of the Sexes (Opposition)

With Venus Opposition Mars, you often experience your own sexuality through projection. You might feel you are the "peaceful" one while your partner is the "aggressive" one, not realizing you are attracting this energy to complete yourself.

Relationships are a battlefield, but an exciting one. You need a lot of friction to feel sparked. A peaceful, quiet relationship often feels "dead" to you.

The Conjunction: The Charismatic

With Venus Conjunct Mars, the Lover and the Warrior are one. You are incredibly magnetic. You have a "unisex" quality—men with this aspect have a soft, artistic side; women have a strong, assertive side.

You throw yourself into love affairs with total abandon. You are an impulsive lover and spender. You love "hard." Creative expression is essential for you—without it, this energy can turn into relationship obsession.

Integration: Healthy Passion

Healing Venus-Mars tension is about uniting your heart and your libido.

  • Own Your Desire: Don't project your aggression or your neediness onto your partner. Accept that you are the one with the desire.
  • Creative Outlet: Paint, dance, build, write. Channel the sexual energy into creative energy.
  • Patience: Learn to wait. The square/opposition often wants instant gratification. True love takes time.
