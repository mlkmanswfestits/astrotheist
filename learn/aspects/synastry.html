Synastry Aspects

The Alchemy of Two Souls

Synastry is the art of comparing two birth charts to see how individuals interact. When your planet falls on someone else's planet or angle, it triggers a specific psychological response. Synastry doesn't predict "if" you will stay together, but "how" the energy will feel between you.

The Golden Links of Compatibility

Sun-Moon Contacts

The "Divine Marriage" aspect. When one person's Sun conjuncts another's Moon, there is a natural flow of vitality and emotional nurturing. The Moon person "gets" the Sun person's light, and the Sun person provides the Moon person with identity and purpose.

Venus-Mars Contacts

The classic spark of sexual chemistry. Venus represents what we find beautiful/valuable, and Mars represents how we pursue and desire. A conjunction or opposition between these two creates an immediate, magnetic attraction.

Saturn: The Relationship Glue

While many fear Saturn, in synastry, it is essential for longevity. Saturn aspects (conjunctions, trines) between charts indicate commitment, duty, and the "glue" that keeps a couple together through hard times. Without Saturn, a relationship may be exciting but fleeting.

Personal Angles & Planets

One of the most powerful triggers in synastry is when someone's planet conjuncts your Ascendant (Persona) or Descendant (Relationship House).

  • Planet on Ascendant: You feel this person has a visceral impact on your very being.
  • Planet on Descendant: They embody exactly what you seek in a partner (the "Missing Half").

Karmic Contacts

Aspects involving the South Node or Vertex often feel fated or destined. You may feel you've known this person before. These connections are powerful for growth but often involve significant lessons (karmic debt) that must be resolved.
