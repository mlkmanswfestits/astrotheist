Synastry Aspects by Sign

Understanding Relationship Compatibility Through Planetary Connections

Synastry is the astrology of relationships. When two charts interact, planets form aspects across the charts, creating a unique energetic dialogue. Understanding these cross-chart aspects reveals the chemistry, challenges, and growth potential in any relationship—romantic, platonic, or professional.

This section explores synastry aspects organized by planet and sign. Each page examines how a specific planet in one person's chart interacts with planets in different signs in another person's chart, revealing the nuances of compatibility beyond simple Sun sign matching.
