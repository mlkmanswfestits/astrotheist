The Sun

Core Identity • Vitality • Spirit

The Sun is the center of your astrological being. It represents your conscious ego, your will to live, and your creative life force. Just as the Sun is the center of our solar system, the astrological Sun is the center of your personality—the "I AM" that integrates all other parts of your chart.

Mythology & Symbolism

In almost every culture, the Sun represents the supreme deity, the source of light and life. In Greek mythology, it's Apollo—god of truth, prophecy, healing, and the arts. Symbolically, the Sun represents the divine spark within us, the hero's journey toward self-realization, and the father archetype. Its glyph is a circle with a dot in the center, representing the infinite spirit focused into an individual point of consciousness.

Core Qualities

  • Rulership: Leo (creative, regal, playful)
  • Exaltation: Aries (at 19°)
  • Detriment: Aquarius
  • Fall: Libra (at 19°)
  • Element: Fire (spirited, active)
  • Glyph: ☉ (the circle of spirit with the point of individual consciousness)

The Sun Represents

  • Identity: Your essential self and how you experience your "I."
  • Vitality: Your physical and spiritual energy levels.
  • Purpose: What you are here to become and express.
  • Authority: Your relationship to power and father figures.
  • Creativity: How you uniquely shine and play.
  • Consciousness: The waking mind and focused awareness.

The Sun rules Leo, the sign of creative self-expression, playfulness, and regal presence. It's exalted in Aries (courage, initiative), in detriment in Aquarius (collective vs individual), and in fall in Libra (self vs other).

The Sun Through the Houses

The house placement shows where you shine and what life area demands your creative expression:

  • 1st House: Identity is central; radiant presence, self-focused
  • 2nd House: Self-worth through resources, values, talents
  • 3rd House: Communication, learning, siblings as identity
  • 4th House: Home, family, roots define you; private self
  • 5th House: Creativity, romance, play; natural Leo placement
  • 6th House: Service, health, daily work as purpose
  • 7th House: Relationships mirror self; identity through others
  • 8th House: Transformation, intimacy, shared power
  • 9th House: Philosophy, travel, higher meaning as core
  • 10th House: Public role, career, legacy; visible self
  • 11th House: Community, ideals, friendships define you
  • 12th House: Spirituality, solitude, unconscious; hidden self

Key Aspects

Sun-Moon

Conscious self meets emotional needs. Conjunction: integrated; opposition: inner tension; square: growth through conflict.

Sun-Mercury

Always close (max 28°). Identity and communication merge. Can indicate strong mental focus.

Sun-Venus

Self-expression through beauty, love, values. Charismatic, artistic, relational.

Sun-Mars

Willpower and action unite. Courageous, assertive, competitive. Square: impulsive; trine: confident action.

Sun-Jupiter

Expansive self, optimistic, generous. Risk of inflation or overconfidence.

Sun-Saturn

Disciplined self, serious, responsible. Can indicate father wounds or delayed recognition. Maturity through time.

Sun-Uranus

Unique, rebellious, innovative. Need for freedom and authenticity. Sudden changes.

Sun-Neptune

Spiritual, artistic, compassionate. Boundary issues, identity confusion, or mystical gifts.

Sun-Pluto

Powerful, transformative, intense. Themes of control, death/rebirth, shadow integration.

Shadow & Growth

Shadow: Ego inflation, narcissism, need for constant validation, dominating others, inability to share spotlight.

Growth: Authentic self-expression without needing approval, generosity, creative courage, healthy pride, inspiring others through your light.
