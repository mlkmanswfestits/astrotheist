The Moon

Emotions • Instincts • Nurturing • Inner Child

The Moon represents your emotional landscape. It governs your instincts, your unconscious habits, and how you need to be nurtured to feel safe. While the Sun is your conscious self, the Moon is your inner world—your private self that only those closest to you truly see.

Mythology & Symbolism

The Moon (Artemis, Selene, or Diana in mythology) represents the divine feminine, the mother, and the soul. It reflects the light of the Sun, just as our emotions often reflect our conscious experiences. The cycles of the Moon—waxing, waning, full, and new—mirror the fluctuating nature of our moods and the biological rhythms of life. Its glyph is the crescent moon, symbolizing the soul's receptivity.

Core Qualities

  • Rulership: Cancer (nurturing, protective, deep)
  • Exaltation: Taurus (at 3°)
  • Detriment: Capricorn
  • Fall: Scorpio (at 3°)
  • Element: Water (emotional, intuitive)
  • Glyph: ☽ (the crescent of the soul)

The Moon Represents

  • Emotions: Your inner feelings and how you react emotionally.
  • Instincts: Your automatic responses and gut feelings.
  • Nurturing: How you care for others and how you need to be cared for.
  • Security: What you need to feel safe and grounded.
  • The Past: Your memories, childhood, and roots.
  • The Inner Child: Your vulnerable, non-rational self.

The Moon rules Cancer, the sign of home, family, and emotional depth. It's exalted in Taurus (emotional stability), in detriment in Capricorn (emotions vs control), and in fall in Scorpio (emotional intensity).

♈ Aries: Impulsive emotions, needs independence, quick to react
♉ Taurus: Stable emotions, needs comfort, sensual nurturing
Gemini: Changeable moods, needs variety, mental processing
♋ Cancer: Deep feelings, needs security, natural nurturer
♌ Leo: Dramatic emotions, needs recognition, warm heart
♍ Virgo: Analytical feelings, needs order, helpful nature
♎ Libra: Harmonious emotions, needs balance, relational
♏ Scorpio: Intense feelings, needs depth, transformative
♐ Sagittarius: Optimistic moods, needs freedom, philosophical
♑ Capricorn: Controlled emotions, needs structure, reserved
♒ Aquarius: Detached feelings, needs space, humanitarian
♓ Pisces: Empathic emotions, needs solitude, dreamy

The Moon Through the Houses

  • 1st House: Emotions on display; sensitive, moody, nurturing persona
  • 2nd House: Security through resources; emotional attachment to possessions
  • 3rd House: Emotional communication; close to siblings
  • 4th House: Natural placement; deep connection to home and family
  • 5th House: Emotional creativity; needs play and romance to feel safe
  • 6th House: Emotions tied to work and health; needs routine for peace
  • 7th House: Needs partnership for emotional security; sensitive to others' moods
  • 8th House: Intense emotional depths; needs intimacy and transformation
  • 9th House: Philosophical emotions; needs travel or learning for comfort
  • 10th House: Public emotions; needs recognition; career as security
  • 11th House: Needs community and friends; humanitarian feelings
  • 12th House: Hidden emotions; needs solitude and spiritual connection

Key Aspects

Moon-Sun

Integration of inner needs and outer purpose. New Moon (conjunction): seed potential; Full Moon (opposition): clarity or tension.

Moon-Mercury

Communication of feelings. Thoughts and emotions integrated or at odds. Rationalizing feelings.

Moon-Venus

Emotional harmony and affection. Needs beauty and comfort. Social charm and emotional warmth.

Moon-Mars

Emotional reactivity. Assertive feelings, protective instincts. Square: volatility; trine: courageous emotions.

Moon-Jupiter

Emotional expansion and optimism. Generous feelings, need for growth. Can indicate emotional excess.

Moon-Saturn

Emotional discipline and restraint. Needs structure for security. Can indicate emotional coldness or responsibility.

Moon-Uranus

Emotional independence and sudden shifts. Needs freedom and variety. Erratic moods or unique insights.

Moon-Neptune

Emotional sensitivity and idealism. High empathy, boundary issues. Dreamy or confused feelings.

Moon-Pluto

Emotional intensity and transformation. Deep intimacy, power struggles. Compulsive feelings or healing power.
