Venus

The Lover • Beauty • Values • Pleasure

Venus is the planet of love, beauty, and values. It governs how you attract, relate, find pleasure, and what you value most. Venus is the lover, the artist, and the diplomat. This is your capacity for connection, your aesthetic sense, and your relationship to money and self-worth.

Mythology & Symbolism

Venus (Aphrodite in Greek) is the goddess of love and beauty, born from sea foam. She represents attraction, pleasure, harmony, and the arts. Venus energy is magnetic, sensual, and relationship-oriented. The morning star and evening star, Venus symbolizes both the light of love and the shadow of desire. She governs everything that brings joy and connection.

Core Qualities

  • Rulership: Taurus (sensual, material) and Libra (relational, aesthetic)
  • Exaltation: Pisces (at 27°)
  • Detriment: Scorpio and Aries
  • Fall: Virgo (at 27°)
  • Element: Earth (Taurus) and Air (Libra)
  • Glyph: ♀ (mirror of the goddess, circle of spirit above cross of matter)

Venus Represents

  • Love: How you give and receive affection, romantic style
  • Attraction: What you're drawn to, how you attract others
  • Beauty: Your aesthetic sense, what you find beautiful
  • Values: What matters most to you, your worth system
  • Pleasure: How you enjoy life, what brings you joy
  • Money: Your relationship with resources and material comfort
  • Relationships: How you relate, partnership style
  • Art: Creative expression, artistic talents

Venus Through the Signs

♈ Aries: Passionate, impulsive love. Attracted to independence.
♉ Taurus: Sensual, loyal, grounded love. Values stability.
♊ Gemini: Intellectual, playful love. Needs mental stimulation.
♋ Cancer: Nurturing, emotional love. Values home and security.
♌ Leo: Dramatic, generous love. values admiration.
♍ Virgo: Practical, service-oriented love. Values competence.
♎ Libra: Harmonious, diplomatic love. Values fairness.
♏ Scorpio: Intense, transformative love. Values depth and power.
♐ Sagittarius: Adventurous, free-spirited love. Values growth.
♑ Capricorn: Serious, traditional love. Values maturity.
♒ Aquarius: Unconventional, friendly love. Values uniqueness.
♓ Pisces: Compassionate, romantic, unconditional love.

Venus Through the Houses

  • 1st House: Attractive persona, charming, values self-expression
  • 2nd House: Values material security, earns through beauty/art
  • 3rd House: Charming communication, values learning
  • 4th House: Beautiful home, values family harmony
  • 5th House: Romantic, creative, values pleasure and joy
  • 6th House: Values health and service, pleasant work environment
  • 7th House: Partnership-oriented, attracts harmonious relationships
  • 8th House: Intense intimacy, values transformation and shared resources
  • 9th House: Values philosophy and travel, attracted to foreign cultures
  • 10th House: Artistic career, diplomatic public image
  • 11th House: Values friendship and community, social charm
  • 12th House: Hidden love, values spirituality and compassion

Venus Retrograde

Venus retrogrades every 18 months for about 40 days. During this time:

  • Relationships: Review, reassess, reconnect with exes
  • Values: Question what truly matters to you
  • Money: Reconsider spending, financial reassessment
  • Beauty: Avoid major appearance changes (haircuts, cosmetic procedures)
  • Opportunity: Heal relationship patterns, clarify values

Natal Venus Retrograde: If you were born with Venus retrograde, you internalize love and beauty. May struggle with self-worth or receiving love. Unique aesthetic sense. Deep capacity for love once you learn to value yourself.

Key Aspects

Venus-Sun

Charming identity, creative self-expression. Values aligned with purpose. Can be vain or self-indulgent.

Venus-Moon

Emotional harmony, nurturing love. Feelings and values aligned. Needs beauty and comfort for emotional security.

Venus-Mercury

Charming communication, artistic thinking. Diplomatic speech. Can avoid difficult conversations.

Venus-Mars

Passionate desire, artistic action. Attraction and pursuit aligned. Strong sexuality. Can be impulsive in love.

Venus-Jupiter

Generous love, expansive values. Lucky in relationships and money. Can overindulge or be excessive.

Venus-Saturn

Serious love, traditional values. Loyal but cautious. Can struggle with self-worth or feel unlovable. Commitment-oriented.

Venus-Uranus

Unconventional love, unique aesthetics. Sudden attractions, need for freedom. Can be erratic or commitment-phobic.

Venus-Neptune

Idealistic love, artistic sensitivity. Compassionate, romantic. Can be delusional, escapist, or attract deception.

Venus-Pluto

Intense love, transformative values. Magnetic attraction, obsessive relationships. Can be jealous, possessive, or manipulative.

Jungian Interpretation

Archetype: The Lover, The Artist, The Anima (feminine principle)

Function: Venus represents the soul's capacity for connection, beauty, and value. It's the principle of attraction and relatedness.

Shadow: Vanity, superficiality, codependency, materialism

Integration: Balancing self-love with other-love, pleasure with discipline, beauty with substance

Venus & Self-Worth

Venus is deeply connected to self-worth. How you value yourself determines what you attract and accept in relationships. Challenging Venus placements (Aries, Virgo, Scorpio) or aspects (Saturn, Pluto) often indicate self-worth wounds that require healing.

Shadow Work for Venus

Questions to explore:

  • Do I truly value myself, or do I seek validation externally?
  • Where do I compromise my values for love or acceptance?
  • How can I balance giving and receiving in relationships?
  • What does beauty mean to me beyond superficial appearance?
  • Where does my desire for pleasure become escapism?
