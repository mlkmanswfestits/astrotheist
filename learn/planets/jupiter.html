Jupiter

The Great Benefic • Expansion • Wisdom • Luck

Jupiter is the planet of expansion, wisdom, and abundance. It governs how you grow, what you believe in, and where you find meaning. Jupiter is the teacher, the philosopher, and the benefactor. This is your capacity for optimism, faith, and the pursuit of truth.

Mythology & Symbolism

Jupiter (Zeus in Greek) is the king of the gods, ruler of the sky and thunder. He represents authority, expansion, generosity, and divine law. Jupiter energy is optimistic, abundant, and seeks higher meaning. The largest planet in our solar system, Jupiter symbolizes growth, protection, and the principle of "more is better."

Core Qualities

  • Rulership: Sagittarius (traditional and modern), Pisces (traditional only)
  • Exaltation: Cancer (at 15°)
  • Detriment: Gemini and Virgo
  • Fall: Capricorn (at 15°)
  • Element: Fire (Sagittarius) and Water (Pisces)
  • Glyph: ♃ (crescent of soul rising above cross of matter)

Jupiter Represents

  • Expansion: Growth, abundance, where you go big
  • Wisdom: Philosophy, higher learning, truth-seeking
  • Luck: Good fortune, blessings, opportunities
  • Faith: Belief systems, religion, optimism
  • Meaning: Purpose, significance, the bigger picture
  • Travel: Long journeys, foreign cultures, exploration
  • Teaching: Sharing knowledge, mentorship, guidance
  • Generosity: Giving, abundance, benevolence

Jupiter Through the Signs

♃ Jupiter in Aries

Pioneering faith, courageous expansion. Believes in action and independence. Lucky through initiative. Can be reckless or overconfident.

♃ Jupiter in Taurus

Material abundance, grounded wisdom. Believes in stability and pleasure. Lucky with money. Can be excessive or indulgent.

♃ Jupiter in Gemini (Detriment)

Intellectual expansion, curious learning. Believes in communication and variety. Lucky through connections. Can be scattered or superficial.

♃ Jupiter in Cancer (Exaltation)

Jupiter exalted—emotional wisdom, nurturing abundance. Believes in family and protection. Lucky through care. Generous and compassionate.

♃ Jupiter in Leo

Creative expansion, confident faith. Believes in self-expression and joy. Lucky through generosity. Can be arrogant or excessive.

♃ Jupiter in Virgo (Detriment)

Practical wisdom, analytical expansion. Believes in service and improvement. Lucky through work. Can be critical or pessimistic.

♃ Jupiter in Libra

Diplomatic expansion, harmonious wisdom. Believes in fairness and partnership. Lucky through relationships. Can be indecisive.

♃ Jupiter in Scorpio

Deep wisdom, transformative expansion. Believes in truth and power. Lucky through crisis. Can be obsessive or extreme.

♃ Jupiter in Sagittarius (Domicile)

Jupiter at home—philosophical, adventurous, optimistic. Believes in freedom and truth. Natural teacher and traveler. Can be preachy or excessive.

♃ Jupiter in Capricorn (Fall)

Strategic expansion, disciplined wisdom. Believes in structure and achievement. Lucky through hard work. Can be pessimistic or rigid.

♃ Jupiter in Aquarius

Progressive wisdom, humanitarian expansion. Believes in equality and innovation. Lucky through community. Can be detached or rebellious.

♃ Jupiter in Pisces (Traditional Domicile)

Spiritual wisdom, compassionate expansion. Believes in unity and transcendence. Lucky through faith. Can be escapist or delusional.

Jupiter Retrograde

Jupiter retrogrades for about 4 months each year. During this time:

  • Growth: Internal expansion, spiritual development
  • Beliefs: Question faith, reassess philosophy
  • Opportunity: Review goals, reconsider meaning

Natal Jupiter Retrograde: Internalized wisdom, philosophical depth. May struggle with optimism but develops profound faith through experience.

Key Aspects

Jupiter-Sun

Optimistic identity, generous nature. Confident, lucky. Can be arrogant or excessive.

Jupiter-Moon

Emotional abundance, nurturing wisdom. Generous feelings. Can be overindulgent.

Jupiter-Saturn

Balanced expansion and discipline. Realistic optimism. Can feel conflicted between growth and restriction.

Jupiter-Uranus

Sudden luck, innovative wisdom. Adventurous, progressive. Can be reckless or unstable.

Jupiter-Neptune

Spiritual expansion, idealistic faith. Compassionate, visionary. Can be delusional or escapist.

Jupiter-Pluto

Powerful expansion, transformative wisdom. Obsessive growth. Can be extreme or fanatical.

Jungian Interpretation

Archetype: The Sage, The Teacher, The King

Function: Jupiter represents the Self's drive toward meaning, expansion, and wholeness.

Shadow: Excess, arrogance, dogmatism, overconfidence

Integration: Balancing expansion with discipline, optimism with realism

Shadow Work for Jupiter

Questions to explore:

  • Where does my optimism become denial?
  • How can I expand without overextending?
  • Are my beliefs truly mine or inherited?
  • Where does generosity become enabling?
  • How can I find meaning without dogma?
