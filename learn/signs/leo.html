Leo

Fixed Fire • Jul 23 - Aug 22

The King • The Creator • The Performer

Leo is the fixed fire sign—confident, creative, and radiant. Ruled by the Sun, Leo seeks self-expression, recognition, and creative joy. This is the king who leads with heart, the creator who brings visions to life, and the performer who shines on life's stage.

Mythology & Symbolism

The Lion is the king of beasts, representing courage, nobility, and solar power. In Greek mythology, the Nemean Lion was invincible, slain by Hercules as his first labor. Leo embodies the hero's journey of claiming one's power and expressing authentic self with courage and generosity.

Core Traits

  • Element: Fire (spirit, creativity, inspiration)
  • Modality: Fixed (stable, persistent, determined)
  • Ruler: Sun (vitality, identity, creative power)
  • Detriment: Uranus (individuality vs. rebellion)
  • Fall: Saturn (joy vs. restriction)

Strengths

  • Confident, radiating self-assurance and charisma
  • Creative, expressing unique vision and artistry
  • Generous, giving warmth and encouragement freely
  • Loyal, fiercely devoted to loved ones
  • Courageous, facing challenges with bravery
  • Playful, maintaining childlike joy and spontaneity

Shadow & Challenges

  • Arrogant, excessive pride and self-importance
  • Attention-seeking, needing constant validation
  • Domineering, wanting to control and be center stage
  • Dramatic, exaggerating emotions and situations
  • Stubborn, refusing to admit mistakes or change
  • Vain, overly concerned with appearance and image

Planets in Leo

Sun in Leo (Domicile): Core identity rooted in creative self-expression and leadership. Natural performer.

Moon in Leo: Emotional security through recognition and creative expression. Needs to feel special.

Venus in Leo: Love through grand gestures and loyalty. Romantic, generous, dramatic in relationships.

Mars in Leo: Action through creative courage and confident assertion. Heroic, proud energy.

Critical Degrees

  • 0° Leo: Pure fire energy, creative initiation, leadership
  • 13° Leo: Creative mastery, performance power
  • 21° Leo: Royal star Regulus proximity—kingship, success, nobility
  • 29° Leo: Anaretic degree—creative completion, urgent self-expression

Decans

1st Decan (0-10°): Saturn sub-ruler. Disciplined creativity, responsible leadership, earned authority.

2nd Decan (10-20°): Jupiter sub-ruler. Generous expression, expansive creativity, philosophical leadership.

3rd Decan (20-30°): Mars sub-ruler. Courageous action, warrior creativity, heroic self-expression.

Jungian Interpretation

Archetype: The King/Queen, The Hero, The Child

Function: Leo represents the Self's drive toward creative self-expression and the integration of personal power with generosity.

Shadow: Narcissism, tyranny, need for constant validation, creative blocks from fear of judgment

Integration: Balancing confidence with humility, leadership with service, self-expression with receptivity.

Shadow Work

  • Where does my need for recognition become desperate validation-seeking?
  • Am I leading or dominating?
  • How can I shine without dimming others?
  • Where does my pride block authentic connection?
  • Can I be generous without needing applause?
