The 36 Decans

Adding Precision and Nuance to the Zodiac

Every zodiac sign covers 30° of the sky. To add more detail, astrologers divide each sign into three 10° segments called decans. Each decan has its own "sub-ruler" from the same element, which flavors how the sign's energy is expressed.

The Chaldean Order vs. Triplicity System

Our system uses the Triplicity Method, which is most common in psychological astrology. It assigns sub-rulers based on the other signs of the same element. For example, Aries (Fire) is ruled by Mars, but its second decan is sub-ruled by Leo (Sun) and its third by Sagittarius (Jupiter).

Decan Breakdown Example: Fire Signs

Sign 1st Decan (0-10°) 2nd Decan (10-20°) 3rd Decan (20-30°)
Aries ♈ Mars (Pure Aries) Sun (Leo side) Jupiter (Sag side)
Leo ♌ Sun (Pure Leo) Jupiter (Sag side) Mars (Aries side)
Sagittarius ♐ Jupiter (Pure Sag) Mars (Aries side) Sun (Leo side)

Why Decans Matter

Decans explain why an Aries born in late March feels different from an Aries born in mid-April.

The 1st Decan is the sign at its most raw and powerful.
The 2nd Decan brings in the social or creative qualities of the next sign.
The 3rd Decan brings in the spiritual or expansive qualities of the final sign in that element's group.
