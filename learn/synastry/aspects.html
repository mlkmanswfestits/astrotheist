Synastry Aspects

The geometric language of relationship chemistry.

Inter-Aspect Description The Experience
Sun-Moon
Conjunction / Trine
 The "Marriage Aspect." The core identity (Sun) aligns with the emotional needs (Moon). A sense of natural belonging and mutual support. One person "shines" while the other provides a "home."
Sun-Moon
Conjunction / Trine / Sextile
 The "Marriage Aspect." The core identity (Sun) aligns with the emotional needs (Moon). A sense of natural belonging and mutual support. One person "shines" while the other provides an emotional "home." The foundation of long-term cohabitation.
Sun-Moon
Square / Opposition
 Internal vs. External needs. The conscious goals of one clash with the emotional habits of the other. Tension between "who we are" and "what we need to feel safe." Requires conscious negotiation to avoid emotional drain.
Moon-Moon
Conjunction / Trine
 Emotional Resonance. Sharing the same "inner weather." Intuitive understanding of moods. Similar domestic habits and ways of nurturing. "Soulmate" comfort.
Mercury-Mercury
Square / Opposition
 Mental Friction. Different processing speeds or communication styles. Frequent misunderstandings. One person thinks linearly while the other thinks intuitively. "We speak different languages."
Venus-Venus
Trine / Sextile
 Shared Values. Similar tastes in art, luxury, and social interaction. Natural agreement on how to spend money and relax. Harmonious social life and aesthetic preferences.
Venus-Mars
Conjunction / Square / Opposition
 Classic erotic attraction. The "Lover-Warrior" dynamic. High erotic charge and pursuit. Squares create intense, sometimes volatile friction that keeps the spark alive through "creative tension."
Mars-Mars
Trine / Sextile
 Synchronized Energy. How you get things done together. Efficient teamwork. Similar sex drive and physical activity levels. Playing on the "same team."
Saturn-Sun
Conjunction / Square
 The Stabilizer or the Critic. Saturn brings the weight of reality to the Sun person. Initially feels like "the one who keeps me grounded." Later can feel like "the one who judges me." Essential for duration through commitment.
Saturn-Moon
Conjunction / Square
 Emotional Responsibility. The "Parent-Child" shadow. The Saturn person provides structure for the Moon person’s emotions, but can also dampen their emotional expression. A very "heavy" binding chord.
Uranus-Venus
Conjunction / Opposition
 The Electric Spark. Sudden, unconventional, or unstable love. Love at first sight. Unusual relationship structures. High excitement but prone to "ghosting" or sudden endings if space isn't maintained.
Neptune-Sun
Trine / Sextile
 Spiritual Muse. Idealization and divine connection. Feeling "meant to be" in a spiritual sense. Deep empathy and psychic connection. A beautiful, "gentle" bond.
Pluto-Moon
Conjunction / Square / Opposition
 Psychological Depth-Charge. Emotional intensity and rebirth. Deeply transformative. Can lead to "emotional possession" and power struggles. Triggers early childhood wounds for healing.
North Node-Sun
Conjunction
 Destined Growth. The Sun person illuminates the Node person's future path. Feeling that the relationship is pulling you toward your higher purpose. A "fated" catalyst for life changes.
South Node-Moon
Conjunction
 Past Life Familiarity. Emotional comfort that spans lifetimes. "I've known you forever." Immediate trust and safety. Risk of staying in the "comfort zone" and not evolving.
Lilith-Venus
Conjunction / Opposition
 Raw Magnetism. The forbidden or untamed attraction. Magnetic, primal draw that often breaks social norms. Can be obsessive, dark, and deeply intoxicating.
Jupiter-Sun
Conjunction / Trine
 The Great Benefic. Expansion, luck, and shared joy. One person naturally "lifts" the other. Optimism, travel, and mutual growth. A very "lucky" connection for abundance.
Chiron-Venus
Any Major Aspect
 The Wounded Healer. Love that triggers old hurts to fix them. Relationship that feels like "healing work." Deep vulnerability. One person may play the "healer" role for the other’s romantic wounds.
