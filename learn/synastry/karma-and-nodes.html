Karma & Nodes

Soulmates & Past Lives

When the Lunar Nodes are involved in synastry, we are looking at something beyond simple chemistry. These are "Growth Contracts."

The Nodes: The Axis of Fate

In synastry, the Lunar Nodes acts as a "magnetic track." When another person's personal planets aspect your Nodes, you are physically and spiritually pulled into their orbit.

The South Node: Past Life Ties

Activation of the South Node feels like instant safety. You "know" this person. There is an immediate language between you. However, these relationships can be draining if the couple stays stuck in the South Node. These are often relationships from the past that have come back for one final lesson or to "settle a debt."

The North Node: Future Bound

One person's planet on another's North Node pulls them toward their evolution. It is often uncomfortable, as it forces you into new behaviors, but it is deeply fulfilling. This is the hallmark of a partner who makes you a "better person" by challenging your status quo.

The Vertex: Fated Encounters

The Vertex is often called the "Third Angle" of the chart. It is an auxiliary Descendant that acts as a point of destiny. When your partner’s planet (especially Sun, Moon, or Venus) is exactly conjunct your Vertex, you may feel that your life has reached a "Before and After" milestone. These connections are nearly impossible to avoid; the universe simply conspires to bring you together.

Karmic Asteroids: Eros & Psyche

Beyond the planets, several asteroids play a vital role in deep-soul connections:

  • Asteroid Psyche (1632): Represents the soul's journey and psychological vulnerability. When Psyche is hit in synastry, there is a "telepathic" or intuitive link.
  • Asteroid Eros (433): Represents the erotic and passionate drive. Eros-Mars aspects are the signature of a "wild," primal attraction.
  • Asteroid Karma (3811): Often highlights exactly where the past-life link is located. In the 4th house, it may be a family link; in the 10th, a professional legacy.

A Note on "Karmic": Not all karmic relationships are meant to last forever. Some are "contracts of completion"—they exist to help you resolve a specific pattern so you can move forward more freely.
