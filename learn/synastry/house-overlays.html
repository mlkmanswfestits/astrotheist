House Overlays

Where your planets light up their life areas.

1st

The House of Self

When someone's planets fall in your 1st house, there is an immediate, visceral impact. You feel their energy physically. This overlay creates instant recognition. If their Sun is here, they "shine" directly on you, making you feel seen and energized. If their Saturn is here, you might feel judged or restricted by their presence. The Planet person is often very focused on the House person's appearance and personality.

 2nd

The House of Values

This overlay shifts the focus to security, money, and self-worth. It is common in long-term marriages where shared resources are key. The House person may feel that the Planet person is a "resource" or brings stability. Conversely, if Mars is here, they might argue about spending. It’s a very grounded, practical connection that determines if you can actually build a life together.

 3rd

The House of Communication

The "Best Friend" overlay. Planets here stimulate intellectual curiosity and conversation. You likely share similar hobbies or ways of thinking. The House person finds the Planet person easy to talk to and may feel like they are "siblings" in spirit. Great for mental rapport and keeping the relationship interesting through dialogue and short trips.

 4th

The House of Home

A deep, psychological and domestic bond. You feel like family almost instantly. This is the "living together" overlay. The House person feels a sense of safety and "at-home-ness" with the Planet person. However, since the 4th house represents our roots and childhood, this overlay can also trigger old family dynamics and early-life patterns that need healing.

 5th

The House of Pleasure

The "Romance and Dating" overlay. This creates playfulness, creativity, and sexual chemistry. The House person sees the Planet person as someone they can have fun with. If your partner's Sun or Venus falls in your 5th, you likely find them incredibly attractive and enjoyable to be around. It is the signature of the "honeymoon phase" and shared joy.

 6th

The House of Service

The "Daily Grind" overlay. This is about routines, work, and health. It might not sound romantic, but it’s essential for functional relationships. The Planet person helps the House person with their chores or health habits. If poorly aspected, the House person can feel like a "servant" to the Planet person, or that the Planet person is too critical of their lifestyle.

 7th

The House of Partnership

The classic indicator for one-on-one commitment and marriage. The House person views the Planet person as the ideal "Significant Other." There is a natural pull toward cooperation and legally binding unions. You "fit" together in the way a traditional partnership requires. The Planet person feels a strong urge to partner up with the House person specifically.

8th

The House of Intimacy

Intense, transformative, and soul-merging. This house governs shared finances, sex, and deep psychological work. When planets fall here, the relationship is rarely "casual." You trigger each other's deepest fears and desires. The House person may feel "possessed" or profoundly changed by the Planet person. A very magnetic but potentially volatile connection.

9th

The House of Wisdom

The "Teacher and Explorer" overlay. Planets here encourage growth, travel, and philosophy. The House person views the Planet person as an inspiration or a guide. You likely enjoy traveling together or debating big life questions. It’s a relationship that expands your horizons and helps you see the "big picture" of your life path.

 10th

The House of Status

The "Power Couple" overlay. This is about public image, career, and reputation. The Planet person often helps the House person achieve their goals or status. The House person respects the Planet person and may see them as an authority figure. It is an excellent overlay for business partners or couples who want to build a public legacy together.

 11th

The House of Community

The "Friendship and Hopes" overlay. This creates a shared vision for the future. You likely share the same social circles and long-term goals. The House person feels like the Planet person is "on their team" and supports their dreams. It’s a socially-oriented connection that keeps the relationship from becoming too insular.

 12th

The House of the Unconscious

The "Deep Psychic" overlay. This involves unspoken bonds, secrets, and spiritual dissolution. The House person may feel that the Planet person can "see" their hidden parts. It can be incredibly romantic and ethereal, or it can feel elusive and confusing. There is often a sense of "karmic debt" or a mission to heal each other in ways that are hard to put into words.
