About Lux Vega

Helping You Discover Yourself Through the Stars

The Journey

For the last two decades, I have dedicated myself to the study and practice of astrology—not as fortune-telling, but as a profound tool for psychological insight and spiritual transformation.

My approach is rooted in Jungian depth psychology, viewing the natal chart as a map of the psyche, a symbolic language that reveals our archetypal patterns, shadow elements, and path toward individuation.

With a background in actuarial science and multimedia, I bring both analytical precision and creative expression to my astrological work, bridging data-driven insights with artistic communication.

Education & Training

School of Metaphysics

My formal training includes attendance at the School of Metaphysics, where I deepened my understanding of consciousness, symbolism, and the esoteric traditions that underpin astrological wisdom. This foundation allowed me to bridge ancient knowledge with modern psychological frameworks.

Continuous Study

Beyond formal education, I have spent years studying the works of Carl Jung, James Hillman, Liz Greene, and other pioneers who recognized astrology as a legitimate psychological tool. I continue to refine my craft through ongoing research, client work, and personal practice.

How I Help You

My work is about helping you learn about yourself in ways you never imagined possible. Through astrology, I provide insights into your life—your patterns, your gifts, your challenges, and your purpose. This isn't fortune-telling; it's self-discovery that brings genuine healing.

I've walked this path myself. Astrology has been my own tool for healing, helping me understand parts of myself I couldn't see any other way. The insights I gained transformed my life, and now I'm passionate about offering that same gift to others. When you understand why you are the way you are, when you see the cosmic blueprint that shaped you, something profound shifts.

Whether through written reports, consultations, or healing sessions, my goal is to help you see yourself clearly—to recognize your gifts, understand your struggles, and make peace with your story. Your natal chart is a mirror that reflects the archetypal patterns living within you. I help you read that mirror.

I use a combination of software-generated reports and in-depth handmade analysis to ensure you receive both comprehensive data and personalized insight. Every chart is unique, and every soul deserves to be seen in its full complexity.

Why "AstroTheist"?

The name AstroTheist reflects my approach: I honor the divine intelligence of the cosmos without requiring belief in a personal deity. Astrology, to me, is a sacred science—a way of recognizing the numinous patterns that govern existence without dogma or superstition.

I work with seekers, skeptics, and believers alike. All I ask is that you bring curiosity and a willingness to look within.
