To answer your second question - YES, it's absolutely worth learning traditional astrology even if you're comfortable with modern.

Modern astrology is amazing for psychological insight and personal growth. Traditional astrology is amazing for timing and prediction. They complement each other beautifully.

For example, modern astrology might tell you "You have Saturn in the 7th house, so you have lessons to learn about commitment." Traditional astrology might tell you "Your 7th house ruler is in the 10th house, so your spouse will be career-oriented and you'll meet them through work."

Different tools for different questions. I use both in my practice and they work together perfectly.
